Something About Us

LOKAL AKT is one of the most active civil society organizations in the Municipality of Makedonski Brod. We are a non-governmental, non-profit organization dedicated to sustainable local and rural development. Several of our initiatives are proudly supported by the European Union. Our mission is to improve the quality of life in rural areas by encouraging citizen participation, strengthening local cooperation, and promoting environmental responsibility. We strive to provide a clean and healthy environment for all residents, to increase the local population, and to make our community a better place to live. We work closely with citizens, institutions, and partners to address key challenges—especially those related to climate change. One of our current projects focuses on increasing the resilience of the local community to environmental risks, particularly the rising threats of forest fires and landslides along the Treska River.

Find quick answers to your most pressing questions about our organization and initiatives.

What is LOKAL AKT?

LOKAL AKT is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable local development. We focus on fostering cooperation between local institutions and citizens. Our goal is to enhance the quality of life in Makedonski Brod.

How can I help?

You can help by volunteering for our projects or donating to support our initiatives. Every contribution makes a difference in our community. Join us in making a positive impact!

What programs do you offer?

We offer programs focused on youth activism, environmental protection, and social entrepreneurship. Our initiatives aim to address local challenges and promote community engagement. Explore our website for detailed information on each program.

How can I get involved?

Getting involved is easy! You can participate in our events, volunteer for projects, or collaborate with us on initiatives. Check our events calendar for upcoming opportunities.

Where is LOKAL AKT located?

LOKAL AKT is based in Makedonski Brod, where we actively engage with the local community. Our office is open to the public for inquiries and collaboration. Visit us to learn more about our work.
